LET THE SEA SET YOU FREE

A deeper meaning to this purposeful symbol, is that it symbolizes one moving forward from a certain period in life or a safe place that they are in, by setting sail on a new journey. Just how it is lifted from the water of a port when a ship sets sail, an anchor raises an individual up by representing new, brighter adventures, allowing one to look forward of what’s to come and to follow their dreams and goals. A symbol representing strong connections, allows for a stable and secure person, who has strong beliefs and stays true to who they are.

A TREND ON ITS OWN

The popularity of the anchor jewelry trend is continuing to grow over the years. If you have a deep love for the sea, the voice of the sea speaks to your mind and body, and there is no better way to wear this love than through this unique looking symbol. Or if you would like to wear a symbol that represents new adventures and brave decision, seas the day by repping an anchor! A symbol of love as well, the anchor represents the two people who are in love with one another, who keep one another level headed by being committed to each other.
